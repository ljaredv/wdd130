Featured Rafting Excursions

Most Popular

The Salmon River Splash

Duration: Full Day | Difficulty: Class III-IV

Our most celebrated daily excursion takes you through the heart of California's stunning river canyons. You'll tackle legendary rapids like "The Big Drop" and "Devil's Punchbowl," interspersed with calm pools perfect for swimming and wildlife spotting. Includes a riverside deli lunch.

Family Friendly

Canyon Family Float

Duration: Half Day | Difficulty: Class I-II

Perfect for families with younger children, grandparents, or those looking for a relaxing day on the water. This gentle stretch features mild riffles, breathtaking geology, and plenty of opportunities to learn about the local ecosystem from your certified naturalist guide.

Ultimate Adventure

Wilderness Multi-Day Expedition

Duration: 3 Days / 2 Nights | Difficulty: Class IV-V

Leave civilization behind on this premier multi-day wilderness journey. Conquering non-stop technical rapids by day, you will camp on pristine white-sand river beaches by night. Our guides handle all the camp setup and prepare gourmet Dutch-oven meals over the campfire.
